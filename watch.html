Our Heritage

Crafted with
Century-Old Wisdom

Founded in Geneva in 1924, Chronosyn was born from a singular obsession: to create timepieces that transcend mere function and become objects of profound beauty. Every watch that leaves our atelier carries the fingerprint of our master watchmakers.

Our movement specialists undergo a decade of training before setting a single gear. It is this uncompromising standard that has made Chronosyn one of the most coveted names in haute horlogerie.
